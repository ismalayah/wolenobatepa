Alyssa Diaz Height: How tall is Alyssa Diaz?

The Rookie actress Alyssa Diaz stands at 5 ft 6 inches (1.68 m & 168 cm) tall.

Diaz also weighs 54 kg.

Who Is Alyssa Diaz?

Alyssa Diaz is an actress known for playing the role of Celia Ortega in the soap opera “As the World Turns” from 2005 to 2006, and detective Angela Lopez in the series The Rookie (2018 – present).

Born on September 7, 1985, in Northridge, Los Angeles, California, Diaz began acting the mid-2000s, making her TV debut in the medical drama series “Medical Investigation.”

In 2011, Diaz played a lead role in the supernatural drama series “The Nine Lives of Chloe King”, and later went on to starr in several other popular TV shows, including “Army Wives,” “The Unit,” “Ray Donovan,” “CSI: NY,” “The Vampire Diaries, “NCIS: Los Angeles,” “Ray Donovan: The Movie”, and “Narcos Mexico”.

In addition Diaz has appeared in many other films like “Red Dawn”, “Ben 10: Alien Swarm”, “How the Garcia Girls Spent Their Summer”, “The Exit Room” , “Shark Night 3D”, and Batman: The Long Halloween (Part Two), playing voice role of Renee Montoya.

Diaz continues to thrill her fans as she takes on new film projects in the film industry.
