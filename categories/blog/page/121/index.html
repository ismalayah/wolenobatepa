South Florida Commissioner Julianna Clare Strout Lied to Visit Lover in ICE Detention

published on May 24, 2024
A South Florida commissioner pled guilty to lying to get into an ICE detention center to see her lover who was an inmate, telling officials she was a paralegal, prosecutors said on Thursday. Julianna Clare Strout, 36, told reporters she’s eight months pregnant and was visiting her husband, former Major League Baseball pitcher Brayan Villareal. Strout tricked ICE officials into thinking she needed to get in to get legal documents signed by a detainee, as social visits had been suspended in October 2021 because of the pandemic, prosecutors said.
