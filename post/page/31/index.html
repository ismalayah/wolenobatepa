Weepinbell (Japanese: ウツドン Utsudon) is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It evolves from Bellsprout starting at level 21 and evolves into Victreebel when exposed to a Leaf Stone. Biology Weepinbell is a pitcher plant Pokémon with a yellow, bell-shaped body. Above Weepinbell's circular eyes are three small green spots and a small, brown hook-shaped stem. It has wide, gaping mouth with pink lips. On each side of its body is a large, green leaf. From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. Christopher David "Chris" Patton is an American voice actor for the English dub of the Pokémon anime.
