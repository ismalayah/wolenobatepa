How to pronounce actuarial

What does actuarially mean?

1 : of or relating to actuaries. 2 : relating to statistical calculation especially of life expectancy.

What is the pronunciation of actuary?

Here are 4 tips that should help you perfect your pronunciation of ‘actuary‘: Break ‘actuary‘ down into sounds: [AK] + [CHOO] + [UH] + [REE] – say it out loud and exaggerate the sounds until you can consistently produce them.

What’s an actuary do?

What Is an Actuary? An actuary uses math and statistics to estimate the financial impact of uncertainty and help clients minimize risk. With a median salary of over $110,000, the profession has a strong employment outlook and projected job growth, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics.
