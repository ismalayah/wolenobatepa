Gallagher began his career on BBC Radio Kent as a presenter and sports reporter. He was a puppeteer and performer on Playhouse Disney, as well as a character and voice actor for Studio Disney. Gallagher hosted Diggin’ It on GMTV from 2003 until 2005. He was the voice-over for Channel 4’s Unanimous and a regular character on Mighty Truck of Stuff as ‘Pablo’ in 2006. Since 2007, Gallagher has played the title character in Mister Maker. He also played the Robot Rat Catcher and the Mummy in two episodes of Bear Behaving Badly. He has also appeared in Grandpa In My Pocket as ‘Mr Liker Biker’ since 2009. Gallagher frequently appears in pantomimes.          In 2006, he played Wishee Washee in Aladdin at Winchester’s Theatre Royal. In 2007, he returned to the Theatre Royal in Winchester to reprise his role as Silly Billy (Jack’s brother) in Jack and the Beanstalk. He played Buttons in Cinderella at the Buxton Opera House in Derbyshire in 2008. In 2009, he co-starred with George Takei in the title role of Aladdin at the Central Theatre in Chatham, Kent, and in 2011, he co-starred with Toyah Willcox in Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs in St Albans. In 2013, he reprised his role as Billy Trot in a production of Jack and the Beanstalk at the Marlowe Theatre in Canterbury, opposite Samantha Womack. Gallagher was nominated for Best Presenter at the BAFTA Children’s Awards in 2009 for his part in Mister Maker.
